BEAM
Material control & inventory management for field operations.
Real-time visibility and accountability over the materials, tools, and equipment moving across your sites — built for construction, MEP, and small-to-mid oil & gas companies in emerging markets.

 </div>

Who we are
BEAM is a responsive web platform that gives asset-heavy field operations real-time control over what they have, where it is, and who's accountable for it. We replace paper stock books, phone calls, and guesswork with a single transparent system — helping companies cut material waste, stop on-site leakage, and always know exactly what's on every location.

We build for how field operations actually run: plain, site-familiar language; a tool that works on a storekeeper's phone and a manager's desktop alike; and accountability built into every material movement.

The problem we're solving
Field operations lose an estimated 15–30% of material value to theft, waste, and administrative error. The causes are familiar: no real-time view of what's been requested, sent, received, or used; unexplained gaps between what's dispatched and what arrives; slow approvals; and no way to tie losses to a specific person, project, or date.

BEAM digitizes the full material lifecycle, requires explanations for discrepancies, and surfaces irregularities the moment they happen.

What BEAM does
Tracks every material, tool, and piece of equipment across all your sites and stores, in real time.
Makes daily logging effortless — a fast, unified flow that adapts to each item type, built for the field.
Holds people accountable — per-worker tool custody, bulk equipment reconciliation, and mandatory explanations for anything that goes missing.
Controls purchasing and stock — with procurement approval trails and low- and out-of-stock alerts.
Reaches people where they are — SMS, email, and in-app notifications.
Gives every role the view they need — from storekeepers on site to executives in head office.
Who it's for
BEAM mirrors real site structure — Site Storekeepers, Site Engineers, Procurement, Project Managers, and Executives — each with a workspace built around their day-to-day responsibilities.

Status
BEAM is in active development. We're building toward a focused MVP that solves the material-control problems field teams face today.
