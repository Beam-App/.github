# 👋 Who We Are

BEAM is a **responsive web platform** built for companies whose operations happen in the field—not behind a desk.

We give asset-heavy organizations real-time control over their materials, tools, equipment, and inventory by replacing paper stock books, spreadsheets, phone calls, and guesswork with a single transparent system.

Our goal is simple:

> **Every material movement should be visible, traceable, and accountable.**

BEAM is designed around how construction and engineering teams actually work:

- 📱 Works just as well on a storekeeper's phone as on a project manager's desktop
- 🏗️ Uses familiar construction terminology instead of generic ERP language
- 🔍 Makes every inventory movement traceable
- 🤝 Builds accountability into day-to-day operations rather than after-the-fact audits

---

# 🚧 The Problem We're Solving

Field operations commonly lose **15–30% of material value** through:

- 🚨 Theft
- 📦 Material wastage
- ❌ Administrative errors
- 📝 Poor record keeping
- 📞 Phone-based approvals
- 📊 Lack of real-time visibility

These problems create questions that teams struggle to answer:

- Where did the material go?
- Who received it?
- What was actually used?
- Why is stock missing?
- Which project is responsible?

By the time discrepancies are discovered, the information needed to explain them is often gone.

BEAM changes that.

# ⚡ What BEAM Does

## 📦 Inventory & Material Control

- Track materials across multiple projects in real time
- Monitor tools, PPE, consumables, and equipment
- View accurate stock levels instantly
- Record every inventory movement

---

## 🚚 Material Lifecycle

Manage the complete journey of every item:

- Material Requests
- Approvals
- Dispatch
- Receipt
- Usage
- Returns
- Reconciliation

Every movement is permanently recorded.

---

## 🛠 Accountability

BEAM helps organizations know exactly who is responsible for what.

Features include:

- Individual tool assignments
- Bulk equipment reconciliation
- Mandatory discrepancy explanations
- Complete audit history
- Transaction ledger for every movement

## 🛒 Purchasing & Stock Control
